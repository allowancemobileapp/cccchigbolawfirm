# **App Name**: LexProfile

## Core Features:

- Professional Profile: Showcase Professor Chigbo's biography, CV, and expertise.
- Areas of Practice: Detail the areas of legal practice with dedicated pages for Property & Conveyancing, Corporate/Commercial Law, and Arbitration/ADR.
- Publications Library: Curate a searchable list of publications with filters, download links, and detailed pages for each.
- Speaking Engagements: List upcoming and past speaking events with summaries and downloadable materials.
- Contact and Booking: Enable users to contact Professor Chigbo and book consultations through a dynamic form and/or booking widget. Includes a map/office address.
- Legal Commentary Generator: Leverage AI to suggest commentary snippets related to trending legal topics or recent publications, enhancing thought leadership presence on the homepage using a tool.
- Client Success Stories: Showcase anonymized case studies or testimonials to demonstrate expertise and build trust.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy (#0B2545) to convey authority and trust.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F9FB) to ensure readability and create a clean, professional look.
- Accent color: Gold (#CFA23B) to highlight key information and calls to action.
- Headings font: 'Playfair', a serif, for an elegant, fashionable, high-end feel.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif, for a modern and neutral look.
- Use simple, professional icons to represent practice areas and services. The official court logo will serve as the primary site logo.
- Ensure a fully responsive design with clear sections and intuitive navigation, optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- Implement subtle animations such as fade-in effects on content and animated scroll-to-anchor to enhance user engagement without being distracting.
